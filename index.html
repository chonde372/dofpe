About Us

The Development Organization for Peoples' Empowerment (DOFPE) is a Kenyan NGO committed to transforming lives through access to education, healthcare, clean water, and sustainable livelihoods. Led by Executive Director Charles Chonde, our mission is to uplift marginalized communities and break the cycle of poverty.

Our Work

  • Education: Building schools, sponsoring children, and offering training programs.
  • Health: Running mobile clinics, supporting maternal health, and health education.
  • Water & Sanitation: Installing boreholes, water tanks, and hygiene awareness campaigns.
  • Poverty Relief: Distributing food aid, offering vocational skills training, and economic empowerment.
